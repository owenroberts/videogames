Open world vs closed world

Open world and free roam are terms for video games where a player can move freely through a virtual world and is given considerable freedom in regards to how or when to approach particular objectives (Wikipedia).

Examples: Legend of Zelda, Grand Theft Auto, Minecraft

Closed world games limit the player to following a specific story line.

Examples: Super Mario, Space Invaders

World or level design
